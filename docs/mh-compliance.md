# Maharashtra Local Regulations Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing the energy audit scope and tender package.

## Legal and Administrative
- Confirm housing society registration and authorized signatories.
- Verify applicable Maharashtra state and municipal energy-related notifications.
- Maintain latest statutory approvals and NOCs relevant to electrical systems.

## Electrical Safety and Fire Prevention
- Schedule periodic inspection of LT panels, feeders, and earthing systems.
- Validate breaker coordination and protection settings with certified personnel.
- Record thermographic inspection results for critical electrical points.
- Ensure fire detection, alarm, and suppression systems are functional.

## Energy Audit Readiness
- Collect 24 months of electricity bills and RMD (Recorded Maximum Demand) data.
- Compile SLDs, load schedules, and equipment inventory.
- Tag high-consumption loads and operating-hour profiles.
- Define baseline KPIs (kWh, kVA demand, PF, DG usage where applicable).

## Vendor and Documentation Controls
- Appoint BEE-recognized/qualified professionals as required.
- Finalize scope boundaries, exclusions, and deliverables in ToR.
- Keep version-controlled records of meetings, approvals, and revisions.
- Archive final report, recommendations, and implementation tracking sheet.
